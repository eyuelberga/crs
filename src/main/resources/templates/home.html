Welcome to the Crime Report Center

The Crime Report Center allows you to report crimes to the police much faster than the conventional phone system.



Features

Easy Reporting

You can report crimes in your location easly. All you need to do is fill a simple form and we will respond to you as soon as possible.

Fast Response

Because you send us all the information we need in a well organized way, we can also respond to your enquires fast.

Get Informed

Through the News section, you can get informations concerning the police department. We also post missing person reports, stolen vehicle reports and other information that you can, as a citizen contribute to.
